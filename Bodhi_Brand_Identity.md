# Brand & Product Identity: Bodhi
**Project:** Unified Personal "Second Brain" Assistant
**Document Version:** 1.0
**Date:** June 20, 2026

---

## 1. The Core Metaphor: The Living Tree
**Bodhi** (Sanskrit: *Awakening, profound knowing*) shifts the paradigm of personal tracking from a mechanical chore to an organic journey of self-mastery. 

In this ecosystem:
* **The Seed:** A single, unstructured text entry in the Universal Input Portal.
* **The Soil:** The cloud LLM router that receives, parses, and nurtures the raw input.
* **The Tree:** The aggregated database—the true "Second Brain"—where isolated entries grow into a deeply rooted network of self-knowledge, behavioral trends, and actionable insights.

Bodhi assumes that ultimate clarity about one's work-life balance, health, and finances doesn't come from rigid ledgers, but from continuous, gentle awareness.

## 2. Brand Positioning & Messaging

**Core Identity:** A digital space for profound personal insight and effortless awareness.

**Taglines:**
* *Awaken to your habits.*
* *Grow your awareness. Master your time.*
* *Plant the seed of clarity.*

**The Promise:** You are no longer dumping data into a void. You are actively cultivating a system that provides ultimate clarity on how you spend your energy, your time, and your resources.

## 3. User Experience (UX) & Visual Identity
The interface must reflect organic growth rather than corporate spreadsheets.

* **The Vibe:** Calming, grounded, and aspirational. The UI serves as a digital garden that blossoms as the user interacts with it over weeks and months.
* **Visual Language:** * **Color Palette:** Natural tones—deep forest greens, warm earth browns, and soft, off-white backgrounds (e.g., `#FAF8F5`). Avoiding harsh neon reds or greens for "failures" or "successes."
    * **Typography:** Clean, modern serif fonts for reflective journaling aspects, paired with highly readable sans-serif for data dashboards.
    * **Animations:** Fluid, breathing transitions. When an entry is logged, it should feel like it is gently absorbed into the system rather than "submitted."
* **The Relationship:** The app acts as a companion on the journey toward self-improvement. It encourages presence and observation.

## 4. Architectural Alignment: The "Second Brain"

How the PRD's technical features map to the Bodhi philosophy:

* **Universal Input Portal (The Cultivator):** Instead of a fragmented UI, the user just speaks or types their reality. It removes the friction of daily life management.
* **Time & Habit Routing (The Roots):** Retroactive natural language parsing seamlessly captures long-term goals. *Example Input:* "Dedicated an hour to studying system design and AI concepts today, and finished my home workout." Bodhi silently updates the skill-building streak and the fitness log.
* **Financial Tracking (The Leaves):** Captures economic energy effortlessly. *Example Input:* "Set aside funds for my advanced tax and invested in the latest Sovereign Gold Bond tranche." The LLM routes these to the financial pillar without requiring category dropdowns.
* **Daily Journaling (The Canopy):** The reflective diary captures the subjective experience. The asynchronous AI enrichment generates `mood_score` and `summary_snippets`, revealing how the toxic elements of a corporate environment or the joy of engaging in a hobby impact the user's overall wellness over time.
* **Sleep & Restfulness (The Heartwood):** Sleep is the hidden pillar of every metric Bodhi tracks. *Example Input:* "Slept at 11:30 PM, woke up at 7 AM, felt deeply rested." Bodhi captures duration, quality, and timing — correlating rest patterns with next-day productivity, focus scores, and emotional baselines over time.
* **Somatic Logs (The Bark):** The body's signals are data too. *Example Input:* "Had a headache in the afternoon, took an ibuprofen, felt better in an hour." Bodhi logs symptoms, severity, and remedies — surfacing hidden correlations like food intolerances triggering bloating or stress-induced flare-ups.

## 5. The Evolution of Insight
After three months of interacting with Bodhi, the user doesn't just see a dashboard; they see the true shape of their life. 

The AI synthesizes the accumulated data to reveal hidden correlations:
* *"Your consistent daily home workouts over the past month heavily correlate with your highest focus days during your evening study sessions."*
* *"When work stress peaks (noted in journal entries), your spontaneous food delivery expenses increase. Cultivating awareness here might bring more balance."*

Bodhi transforms raw, chaotic daily actions into structured, beautiful enlightenment.
